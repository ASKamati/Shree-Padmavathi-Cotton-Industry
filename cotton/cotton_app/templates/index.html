Our Services

Comprehensive cotton ginning and pressing solutions with advanced technology

Cotton Ginning

Advanced ginning services using Technology Machine on Cotton (TMC) to separate cotton fibers from seeds with precision and efficiency.

Cotton Pressing

Professional cotton pressing services using modern machinery to compact cotton into high-density bales for efficient storage and transport.

Technology Machine on Cotton

State-of-the-art Technology Machine on Cotton (TMC) with Rs.10 Crores investment ensuring highest quality processing standards.

Raw Cotton Processing

Complete raw cotton processing from sourcing to final product with 30+ years of expertise in the cotton industry.

Cotton Bale Supply

Quality Control

Building the Future of Cotton Ginning & Pressing

SHREE PADMAVATI COTTON INDUSTRIES is a Partnership Firm specializing in Cotton Ginning & Pressing. Founded by Mr. Mohan T Astagi and his Father Sri. Tavanappa P Astagi, we have been dedicated to the cotton business for over 30 years.

Our state-of-the-art facility uses Technology Machine on Cotton (TMC) with a substantial investment of Rs.10 Crores. Located at "BASAVA MARG" DURGADEVI TEMPLE, SAUNDATTI DHARWAD-580006, we are committed to delivering excellence in cotton processing.
